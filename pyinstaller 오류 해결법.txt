=================이전 방법(실패)

(pip uninstall pyinstaller)
pip install https://github.com/pyinstaller/pyinstaller/archive/develop.zip

2. 아래 명령어 실행
pyinstaller --hidden-import=pkg_resources.py2_warn --onefile --windowed --icon "C:/Users/SeungWon/PycharmProjects/DCinsideAlarm/icon.ico" --add-data "C:/Users/SeungWon/PycharmProjects/DCinsideAlarm/dc_image.ico;." --add-data "C:/Users/SeungWon/PycharmProjects/DCinsideAlarm/icon.png;." ""
